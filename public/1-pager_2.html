Process

  1. Plan & Define: Fill out a quick intake form detailing your goals, timeline, and must-haves. We’ll jump on a short call if needed to clarify scope. No costs until we lock in the plan and decide to move forward.
  2. Sprint Execution: I work in weekly blocks (around 20 hours each), typically spanning 4–8 weeks. Each sprint covers:
    • Strategy alignment (what matters most this week?)
    • UX/UI design (wireframes, mockups, or interactive prototypes)
    • Coding (iterative development driven by user feedback)
    You get a weekly check-in to review deliverables, clarify next steps, and pivot if necessary. No waiting on a massive final reveal—expect tangible prototypes every step of the way.
  3. Deliver & Evolve: End with a functioning MVP built on React + Tailwind. Perfect for user testing, investor demos, or handing off to a larger team. Need more features later? Re-engage anytime.

Principles

  • Clarity: Transparent pricing and a straightforward process—no hidden fees or runarounds.
  • Focus: Start small, solve a critical problem, and iterate. I cut out the noise so you can move forward fast.
  • Response: Weekly sprints mean weekly feedback. If data or user insights prompt a pivot, we adapt immediately—no wasted cycles.
